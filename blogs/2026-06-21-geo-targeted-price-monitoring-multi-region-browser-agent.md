A hands-on guide to building a multi-region price monitoring agent using Anchor VPN, Python, Playwright, and Pydantic to scrape pricing pages from five countries simultaneously. It shows how competitors price SaaS plans differently across regions (such as Germany versus Australia) and how geo-targeted automation surfaces those differences.
